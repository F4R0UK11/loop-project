The key to the academic justification is showing direct alignment between your home university's degree requirements and the host's offerings. Don't just list classes; map them to specific credits you need to graduate. Also, for France specifically, make sure to highlight any prior language proficiency even if the classes are in English.

I applied to Spain last year! Have backups for your backups. The class registration process there can be chaotic, so your academic justification should include at least 2-3 alternate classes per required slot.
