To apply for an exchange program at AUI, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Maintain a minimum CGPA of 3.0.
  • Have completed at least 60 credits prior to departure.
  • Not be in your final semester.

The application portal typically opens on September 1st for Spring exchanges and February 1st for Fall exchanges. You must submit a letter of motivation and two academic references.
