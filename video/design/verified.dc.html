Do I need to update my carte de séjour if I move off-campus next semester — and how long does it take?

Yes — your carte de séjour is tied to the address on your file, so moving off-campus means updating it. Bring your new lease and a proof of address to the Office of International Programs; they submit the updated file to the Bureau des Étrangers on your behalf Policy. You'll get a récépissé — a temporary receipt — to cover you while the new card is processed. Budget three to four weeks, and go early in the semester: the office gets swamped right after move-in, and the récépissé matters if you plan to travel Lived experience.
